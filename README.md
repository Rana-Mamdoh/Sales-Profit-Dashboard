# Sales-Profit-Dashboard
Interactive Excel dashboard for Sales & Profit analytics across categories, states, and time.
<img width="1600" height="873" alt="dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d413f11b-1023-4ad9-89f7-3a157ddcac12" />

KPIs
- Total Sales: $1,928,888
- Total Profit: $247,961
- Customer Count: 2,319 (2021–2024)
 Features
- Filter by Category, Year, and Month
- Profit by Year breakdown (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)
- Sales by Category (17 categories)
- Top 5 Customers by Profit
- Sales by Month trend
- Customer Count by Year (donut chart)
- Geographic Sales by State (USA map)
  Insights
- Phones & Chairs are the top revenue categories ($279K and $277K)
- Technology shows the strongest profit growth year over year
- Q4 (Nov–Dec) peaks in monthly sales at $234K and $241K
- Tamara Chand is the #1 customer by profit at $8,981
- 2023 had the highest customer count at 638
